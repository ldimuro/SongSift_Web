Song Sift

Song Sift is an application that grabs all of the songs in your library and lets you sort and filter them to create playlists


For every song in Spotify's library, there is a list of attributes used to characterize the song, including:

  • Energy (from 0.0  –  1.0)
  • Happiness (from 0.0  –  1.0)
  • Danceability (from 0.0  –  1.0)
  • Loudness (from -60.0  –  0.0)

Song Sift lets you sort and filter your songs by these attributes and export them directly into a Spotify playlist
